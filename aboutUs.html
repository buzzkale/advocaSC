About advocaSC

What We Do

We are a community-based organization, meaning that our work heavily focuses on homelessness specifically in L.A.. We develop this specialization by hosting panels with guests from organizations such as L.A. Family Housing and Downtown Women's Center and pushing for the policies we believe will benefit our community the most.

Get Involved

Are you passionate about what we're doing? Let us know by filling out the interest form linked here! We are always looking for more members to help us make our vision for a better Los Angeles a reality. We'll help you find a way to participate that best suits you. We're excited to have you join the team!
